Royal Oceanic Bliss sits in Varca, on the quietest stretch of South Goa's Salcete coast — a village of white-sand beaches, seaside chapels and the luxury-resort belt that runs south towards Cavelossim, with Margao's markets and railway station an easy drive inland. This is the Goa of unhurried mornings and empty beaches, fifteen minutes from everything you need.

The collection is deliberately small: eight independent villas across roughly 1.3 acres, each a grand four-bedroom residence of about 4,896 sq ft in a neoclassical idiom — double-height arched windows, columned porte-cochère, terracotta roofs and walled garden courts. Inside, arched living rooms rise two storeys, the master bath is crowned by its own jacuzzi, and every villa keeps its own private pool and covered parking within its walls.

Life beyond your gate is equally considered: a branded entrance arch opens onto paved, palm-lined avenues, landscaped gardens with gazebos, and a residents' clubhouse with a billiards lounge and a fully equipped gymnasium. The project is under construction with completion targeted for December 2027 — villa layouts, specifications, pricing and registration details are shared on enquiry, along with a site visit at a time that suits you.
