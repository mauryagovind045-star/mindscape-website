Caladium is an intimate gated enclave of just nine Portuguese-inspired villas, tucked into the palm-shaded countryside of Vaddo, Siolim. Arched façades, terracotta Mangalore-tiled roofs and hand-finished details blend effortlessly into Goa's landscape, while each home stands on its own generous plot for genuine privacy and space.

Every villa unfolds across three levels around soaring double-height living areas, tall French doors and graceful arched openings that pull in natural light. A rich material palette — lime-finished walls, cement-tile and natural-stone floors, exposed timber beams and imported marble — carries through four en-suite bedrooms, a spa-inspired master and a modular kitchen with premium appliances. Integrated smart-home automation and split air-conditioning are built in throughout.

Outdoors, a private swimming pool with an anti-skid limestone deck sits amid landscaped gardens, gazebos and shaded verandahs made for alfresco dining. With private parking, a staff room and power backup — and Siolim's beaches, riverside and dining moments away — Caladium is equally a graceful family home and a high-yield holiday-let investment.
