Upper Crest is a thoughtfully designed residential tower rising near Muth in Gogol, just off the Margao–Curtorim road — a quiet, well-connected pocket of South Goa moments from Margao town, the Eastern Bypass and Big Foot Circle. Conceived as an exclusive address of only thirty-six three-bedroom homes, it is a fully residential building with no commercial activity, so the privacy and calm of home is never infringed upon.

Every apartment is planned around light and air — windows oriented for maximum sunlight with generous wind channelling, wide balconies, French doors and enclosed utility balconies framing open views. The tower stands over two levels of stilt car parking (P1 + P2) beneath eight residential floors, served by two automatic elevators — one passenger and one service — with power back-up for lifts and common areas, 24-hour CCTV monitoring of key areas and a fire-fighting system throughout.

Interiors are finished to a high standard: vitrified flooring, laminated door shutters, UPVC windows, natural-stone window sills, granite kitchen platforms with stainless-steel sinks, European-style bathroom fittings, modular electrical switches and intercom to security — with provision for air-conditioning, water purifier, washing machine and dishwasher, plus fibre-optic cabling for high-speed internet. Shared amenities include a clubhouse with indoor games, a rooftop sky walking track, a terrace garden promenade, the 'Shunyaa' yoga point and a dedicated activity and party area — an elevated way of everyday living.
