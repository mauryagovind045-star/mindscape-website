Amara is a limited collection of only eight spa villas set among the paddy fields and heritage homes of Socorro — a serene village in North Goa where rolling green fields meet the buzz of Porvorim, just five minutes away. Conceived as soulful sanctuaries rather than mere homes, each residence is a study in biophilic, GRIHA-certified design that reconnects you with nature without asking you to compromise on comfort. Only eight will ever exist.

Every villa is a grand three-bedroom residence rising over four levels, wrapped around a walk-in plunge pool you step into straight from the living room — no barriers, just liquid luxury. Vaulted, dome-topped architecture frames arched windows and wide balconies, while a private lift connects the ground-floor living, dining and kitchen up through en-suite bedrooms to a top-floor skylight lounge, steam room and rooftop jacuzzi open to the stars. A water feature greets you at the entrance, and spa-like bathrooms, a lavish master bath, walk-in wardrobes and a hardwood staircase complete the sense of a private resort.

Villas come semi-furnished with a premium modular kitchen, luxury bathroom fittings, energy-efficient air-conditioning, smart-home automation and 24/7 power back-up. Beyond your door: serene landscaped gardens, a barbecue station, a home theatre, EV charging and secure access with video surveillance — plus chef-on-call and nanny-on-call services for effortless living. This is conscious luxury, uncompromised — an address that teaches the calendar to unwind.
