Casa Solaris takes its name from the sun — and builds the whole home around it. Skylights and expansive glazing pour daylight into the ground-floor living and dining spaces, which open through a veranda to your own pool and garden. Around you: the paddy fields and low hills of Siolim, with Assagao's restaurant belt and the sands of Morjim and Vagator a short drive away.

Only ten villas make up the community, each rising three levels with a private lift — four or five bedrooms depending on the plan, with balconies and terraces at every turn. RERA carpet areas run from 2,238 to 2,963 sq ft (built-up 3,494 to 4,076.5 sq ft) across seven distinct layouts, so there is a plan to fit how you actually live. Every villa carries its own pool filtration, water tanks and STP, three-phase electricals and anti-skid flooring on a quake-resistant RCC frame.

The community is gated with round-the-clock security, and the villas are ready to move — no waiting on possession. The project is RERA-registered (PRGO05242225, Survey No. 244/1-A, Maina–Sodiem, Siolim). Floor plans, specifications and pricing are shared on enquiry, along with a site visit at a time that suits you.
