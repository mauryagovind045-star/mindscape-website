Tamega is a serene residential enclave of just seven homes, set across 2,475 square metres in the coveted village of Marna, Siolim. Each villa is thoughtfully composed to exude quiet luxury and a deep connection to Goa's landscape — framed by open paddy fields and lush countryside, yet minutes from the beaches and bars of the north.

The architecture draws on Goa's Portuguese heritage — earthy tones, timeless proportions and modern comfort in equal measure. Inside, a double-height living area is bathed in natural light and opens through an open-plan layout to a contemporary kitchen and dining space. Three inviting bedrooms, including a master suite, are finished with soft hues and modern en-suite bathrooms with upscale fittings.

Every home is wrapped around its own private infinity pool and landscaped garden, with a generous pool deck for slow Goan evenings. Managed hospitality — from 24/7 concierge and housekeeping to airport transfers and a private butler on request — makes each villa as easy to own as it is to enjoy.
