A landmark residence under construction in Siolim, North Goa — a Portuguese-Mediterranean villa arranged over four levels, from a lower-ground garden suite to a rooftop terrace bar. Arched colonnades, terracotta-tiled roofs and full-height windows frame a private pool and sun deck at its heart.

Five bedroom suites, a home theatre, a spa with steam room, and a passenger lift serving every floor make this the most substantial home in our collection. Set on an 800 sq.m plot with sub-tropical gardens, staff quarters and covered parking.

Currently under construction. Floor plans and the full area statement are available on request; price on application.
