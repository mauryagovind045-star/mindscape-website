Sanctuary at 108 is a private Balinese-inspired villa set just 650 metres from Fatrade Beach, in one of South Goa's most pristine and quietly tucked-away coastal settings. Inspired by the tranquil elegance of Balinese design, the home embraces open spaces, natural light and a seamless connection to the tropical landscape — a rhythm of calm and simplicity, a sanctuary by the sea.

Soft arches and open volumes give way to seamless indoor–outdoor living, finished in natural materials and a warm tropical palette. Full-height windows and skylights flood every room with daylight, framing views of greenery and sky, while landscaped gardens, quiet sit-outs and open courtyards create intimate spaces to relax and entertain amid lush greenery.

The three-bedroom villa sits on a 750 sqm freehold plot, with 250 sqm of built space designed around light and openness and 300 sqm of landscaped gardens. A 9 × 14 metre private pool forms the heart of the home, complemented by detached staff quarters, power backup and security infrastructure for effortless everyday living. Freehold land ownership with a bespoke villa — priced significantly below prevailing luxury market rates, with strong early-buyer potential.
