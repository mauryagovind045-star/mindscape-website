2 & 3 BHK · Ready to Move In

Elite Square is a gated, ready-to-move-in residential enclave of contemporary 2 and 3 BHK apartments set on the fringes of Porvorim, one of North Goa's most upmarket and best-connected addresses. Laid out across four low-rise blocks around landscaped gardens, a fountain plaza and a resort-style swimming pool, it commands open views of gorgeous natural greenery while keeping the city moments away.

The homes are designed around light and space — a spacious living room set apart from the dining area, well-proportioned bedrooms with ample natural light, and private covered terraces that open to the serenity of nature. Apartments come fitted with a modular kitchen, air-conditioning and fans, with 2 BHK homes from ~117 sq m and 3 BHK homes at ~141 sq m of built-up area.

A full suite of amenities serves the whole family — a fully-equipped gymnasium, yoga and meditation zone, badminton and half-basketball courts, indoor games, a children's play area, a party hall and clubhouse, and a senior-citizens' zone. The gated complex adds 24-hour security, CCTV, video phones, power back-up, EV charging and an earthquake-resistant structure. With shopping, malls, schools, banks and hospitals right outside the gate, daily life is effortless. The 2 and 3 BHK homes are ready to move in today — current pricing is shared on request, exclusive of stamp duty, registration and statutory charges.
