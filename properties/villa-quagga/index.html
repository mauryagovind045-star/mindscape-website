A characterful three-bedroom villa in the quiet townlet of Morjim, North Goa, where Portuguese whimsy meets tropical minimalism. Arched windows, wooden-rafter ceilings and hand-glazed zellige bathrooms give every room genuine Goan-Mediterranean character, while a double-height living room opens to field and garden views.

Offered fully furnished and ready to move in. A private courtyard pool, subtropical garden and large terrace complete the home — with round-the-clock concierge, property management and security on hand. Morjim beach is minutes away.
