Raghavan Green Valley is the newest phase of an established gated community in Socorro — the green edge of Porvorim where paddy fields, mesmeric sunsets and birdsong at dawn are part of the address. The site sits just off Guirim junction, minutes between Mapusa and Panaji, with everyday conveniences a short drive away.

The homes are three-and-four-bedroom row villas and independent villas, each rising from stilt-level covered parking through two living floors to a private rooftop terrace — with a lift inside every villa serving all levels. Wide balconies with flower boxes, arched Goan facades and laterite-stone bases give the homes their character; the independent villas add a private garden and plunge pool.

Residents share a large common swimming pool, an amphitheatre set in common greens, a multipurpose court, indoor games and a community hall. The project is built to tread lightly too — waste-water recycling, passive cooling design, groundwater recharging and electric-vehicle charging are all part of the plan. RERA-registered, and the latest chapter of a community that has been loved and lived in for years.
