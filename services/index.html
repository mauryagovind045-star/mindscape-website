Our Services.

Buying or building in Goa runs through a maze of departments — Panchayat, TCP, Health, Municipality, Fire. Our licensed civil engineers walk that maze for you, from first file to final approval.

01 · Property Liaisoning

Approvals & permissions,
handled end to end.

Every construction, renovation or occupancy in Goa needs a chain of clearances — and each authority has its own forms, fees, site inspections and timelines. A single missing document can stall a project for months.

We prepare your file, submit it to the right desk, attend the inspections, and follow up until the approval is in your hand. Because our team reads drawings and land records professionally, queries from the authorities get answered correctly the first time.

Enquire About Liaisoning
What we handle
  • Panchayat — construction licence & NOCs, house numbering, occupancy certificate and related village-level permissions
  • Town & Country Planning (TCP) — zoning information, technical clearance and development permission for your plot or project
  • Health Department — health NOCs and sanitation clearances required for occupancy and trade
  • Municipality — construction licences, trade licences, occupancy certificates and municipal NOCs within city limits
  • Document preparation, submissions, fee payments, inspection coordination and follow-ups till final approval
02 · Fire Liaisoning

Fire NOC & fire-safety
compliance, made simple.

A Fire NOC from the Directorate of Fire & Emergency Services is mandatory for most commercial buildings, guest houses, restaurants and larger residential projects in Goa — and it must be renewed on time to stay valid.

We review your building against the fire-safety norms, point out exactly what needs to be installed or corrected, prepare the application, and coordinate the fire department's inspection — for both fresh NOCs and renewals.

Enquire About Fire NOC
What we handle
  • Fire NOC — new — provisional (during construction) and final NOC applications, prepared and filed
  • Fire NOC — renewal — timely renewals so your licence, trade or occupancy never lapses
  • Liaison with the Directorate of Fire & Emergency Services, Goa
  • Fire-safety compliance review — extinguishers, hydrants, alarms, escapes and signage as per norms
  • Inspection coordination and rectification guidance until clearance is issued
03 · Civil Contracts

Built by the same engineers
who check what we sell.

Construction in Goa fails on supervision, not intent. As licensed civil engineers, we take on select civil contracts where we control the quality ourselves — from foundation to finish.

You get a clear scope, an itemised estimate, branded materials as specified, and an engineer on site — not just a contractor's word. Progress is reported against a schedule you can hold us to.
